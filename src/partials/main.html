Features

  • Transformation Logic

    Wild symbols can replace any regular symbol. The only exception is the Bonus symbol, which remains untouched.

  • Wild Attack Feature

    On the top row, special Wild Attack symbols appear — the only place where they can exist. When one aligns directly above a Demon symbol, the guardian force strikes through, transforming the Demon into a Wild and opening new opportunities for combinations.
